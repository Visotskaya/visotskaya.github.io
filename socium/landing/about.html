About SociumTrade

How the idea was born?

SociumTrade is a product demanded by the market

As bitcoin became increasingly popular over the last couple of years, we saw a rise in retail demand for crypto-related financial services. People with little to no knowledge of cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology wanted to get involved in the market and profit from the meteoric growth of crypto assets. Potential investors were asking about managers who could invest their money for them, but there was no infrastructure in place for individuals to fund traders and have them manage their money. On top of that the exchanges were lacking in use.

Seeing this, our founders decided to offer a solution for the growing crypto space, but also go further. Crypto assets make up a very small amount of money. Our new platform, SociumTrade, was to be architected to enable traders and investors. SociumTrade is a product demanded by the market, and we see a bright future for it and the industry.

Our mission is to equip traders and investors with the most comprehensive suite of instruments for cooperating and profiting from the market.

Our vision

We see a future in which financial markets are made up of individuals engaged in free trade; a future, in which big institutions no longer are the gatekeepers and everyone can interact directly with one another. We see security and transparency becoming the cornerstones of every transaction, and individuals taking control of their financial futures.
We are developing SociumTrade to be the tool that begins to realize that vision

Roadmap

2017
May 2017
Idea
The idea is born to build a platform that would allow direct p2p investment between an individual and a trader, and for traders to procure capital and trade on the platform.
June 2017
Research and Validation
Through research and consultations with market insiders, the idea is validated and expanded to incorporate consortiums and pools and allow for traditional asset classes down the line.
August 2017
Whitepaper 1.0
September 2017
POC and evaluation
2018
March 2018
Whitepaper 2.0
Q2 2018
Company Registered in Malta and Estonia
Q2 2018
SociumTrade MVP
A functional platform that supports user profiles, social connectivity, crypto-asset trading and one-to-one investing.
May 2018
Private-sale
We will be doing a private sale of tokens to selected, vetted investors.
July 2018
Socium Exchange Launch
This will enable users to trade assets and use advanced order types to generate profit in the most efficient way. HEX prototyping.
August 2018
Socium Lend
The launch of the p2p pawnshop tool. Integration with land-based partners to facilitate operations with fiat.

BrokerGram
Initiating the project, public vote for scope of work, contest of the best POC.

Public sale of tokens.

Gift making platform in order to offer modern crypto- presents to the community of enthusiasts.
