Roadmap

2017
May 2017
Idea
The idea is born to build a platform that would allow direct p2p investment between an individual and a trader, and for traders to procure capital and trade on the platform.
June 2017
Research and Validation
Through research and consultations with market insiders, the idea is validated and expanded to incorporate consortiums and pools and allow for traditional asset classes down the line.
August 2017
Whitepaper 1.0
September 2017
POC and evaluation
2018
March 2018
Whitepaper 2.0
Q2 2018
Company Registered in Malta and Estonia
Q2 2018
SociumTrade MVP
A functional platform that supports user profiles, social connectivity, crypto-asset trading and one-to-one investing.
May 2018
Private-sale
We will be doing a private sale of tokens to selected, vetted investors.
July 2018
Socium Exchange Launch
This will enable users to trade assets and use advanced order types to generate profit in the most efficient way. HEX prototyping.
August 2018
Socium Lend
The launch of the p2p pawnshop tool. Integration with land-based partners to facilitate operations with fiat.
August 2018
BrokerGram
Initiating the project, public vote for scope of work, contest of the best POC.
September 2018
ICO
Public sale of tokens.
Oct 2018
Socium Gift
Gift making platform in order to offer modern crypto- presents to the community of enthusiasts.
Q4 2018
SociumTrade Launches Many-to-One Investment
At this point traders will be able to accept investments for a single portfolio from multiple individuals. HEX beta.

Q2 2019
SociumTrade Launches Investment Pools and Trader Consortiums
We will be doing a private sale of tokens to selected, vetted investors.
Q3 2019
FX integration
Traders can benefit from Forex market, having the same terminal and transparency.

ICO voting tool, listing orders
This will enable users to propose new promising products and start crowdfunding straight away with guaranteed listed token.
August 2019
Index development
Develop, launch and manage crypto-asset basket indexes.

Alternative assets
Enable tokenized alternative assets to be traded on the platform, integration with real estate investment platforms.

SociumTrade outbound API
