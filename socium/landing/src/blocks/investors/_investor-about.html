Pools

Socium Trades enables investors to join into pools. These associations allows several participants to give their financial resources into management of another pool participant under the negotiated terms. The head investor, who also allocates his/her capital to the pool, manages all the funds in the pool and usually receives a certain reward for it. The conditions of participation, funds allocation, work rules etc are written in smart contracts.
Pools enables investors to put in their financial resources safely and with minimum efforts and earn passive income.
