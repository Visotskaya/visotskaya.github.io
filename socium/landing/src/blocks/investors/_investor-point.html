Investment process

Socium Trade gathers and stores all information on its users, thus enabling investors to find traders by set criteria.
Such as a trader’s rating, expertise, total number of funds in management, percentage of a trader’s own funds in his/her management, risk rate, profitability etc.

Copy trading

Investors can copy an end-of-period asset allocation of a given trader’s portfolio.
With the advanced instrument kit, investors can decide on the most suitable copying strategy and arrange their portfolio (or proportion of it) according to the portfolio of a selected trader(s).
